Oreintal Trading company gives back to society!


The csr of oriental trading company lies on giving back to the community. They focus on 4 key areas which are education, youth, healthcare, and human services. They do this in various ways such as food banks, classroom mentors, visiting children's hospitals, providing for homeless shelter and giving a hand to others in the community. They finance these wide range of activities through donations which can trace back to more than 5000 donors annually.
